Welcome

Poornima College of Engineering, was established in 2000 with the aim of imparting pragmatic technical education. In its magnificent journey of 18 years, PCE has set benchmarks and reached at new pinnacles in engineering education with dedication, perseverance and devotion. PCE is spearheading its outstanding voyage with motto ‘Success is not a destination, it’s a journey’. Poornima College of Engineering, Jaipur has been consistently ranked 2nd out of 116 Engineering Colleges in QIV Ranking of Rajasthan Technical University, Kota, from the last two academic years. The QIV Ranking is based primarily upon Academics, University results, Placements & Alumni.

Highlights

  • The most preferred Engineering college with 100% admissions through REAP/JEE. It runs seven specializations of engineering (CE, ECE, EE, ME, Civil, IT & Agriculture) at UG level.
  • First private Engineering College in Rajasthan accredited by Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) for regular campus placements.
  • Selected among Top Five Institutions of Rajasthan under TEQIP scheme.
  • An IBM Lab has been setup at Poornima College of Engineering. IBM has provided relevant IBM software & curriculum content in this lab for Internet of Things (IOT), Cloud Computing & Business Intelligence.
  • First NBA Accredited Institution.
  • An institute designated as Centre of Excellence by IBM.
  • Remote Study Centre of IIT Madras, IIT Kharagpur & IIT Bombay.
  • Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) to promote the spirit of entrepreneurship.
  • Highly recognized and renowned among the industrial and corporate sector in India.
  • ASHRAE has approved $5000 for student project-Solar Milk Refrigerator to the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Poornima College of Engineering
  • 8 Patents successfully applied.
  • Several projects sponsored by AICTE & DST, Govt. of Rajasthan.
  • Organized a workshop on Industrial Automation & Embedded Systems (IAES- 2014).
  • Organized a National conference on Advances in refrigeration & Energy Systems in association with Indian Society of Heating Refrigerating and Air Conditioners Engineers (ISHRAE).
